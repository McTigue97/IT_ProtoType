LOI F1 is the biggest online Formula 1 tournament in Ireland

The League Of Ireland F1 is a Formual 1 Championship league where the best gamers in Ireland compete in it. Set up in 2019, LOI F1 is an online Playstation championship event where the best F1 gamers in Ireland battle it out every week during the championship calendar. With over 100 memebers the championships are split into 4 tiers based on the gamers speed and experience. Tier 1 is the highest level of competition you can face as only the elite gamers are up for the challenge. The championships are broken into seasons, with each season commencing and finsihing within 2-3 months. There is usually a mid season break along with a few weeks brake at the end of each season. There have been many champions throughout the season's with some currently being unbeaten. Take a look at our hall of fame and see the legends of the League.
